BIMBHTP

BIMBHTP - an acronym meaning ‘but it might be hard to park‘. Very important to those pursuing a Southern California life-style.

BIMBVHTP - an acronym meaning ‘but it might be very hard to park‘. Particularly applying to visiting Philipp in Venice Beach in the summer.

BIMRBVHTP - an acronym meaning ‘but it might really be very hard to park‘. Typically used when attempting to park near the Sunset 5, just prior to finding a perfect parking space (from which the car will subsequently be towed).
