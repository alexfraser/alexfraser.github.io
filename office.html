The building is close to the Commundo Tagungshotel (Commundo Conference Hotel). You can follow the signs to the hotel, and then look left to see our building, the "Informations Zentrum Informatik".

The door entrance is between the building and the wooden-paneled building next to it, and is unlocked during normal business hours. Go up one floor and then look for office number 1.011 (this should be to your right when you come out of the elevator or stairwell).
