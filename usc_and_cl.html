USC and Computational Linguistics

USC has excellent resources for Computational Linguistics. There is a very strong Natural Language Group at the Information Sciences Institute which has 7 full-time researchers and more than 10 graduate students. On the USC campus, there are many faculty interested in problems related to Computational Linguistics in the Computer Science, Linguistics, and Electrical Engineering departments. Also, USC has recently begun a Masters program in Computational Linguistics, which has increased the number of students working in this area.
